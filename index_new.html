Federal investigations show 10–15% of ABA billing is fraudulent. With billions in annual spending, the losses are staggering.

Common Fraud Schemes We Prevent

  • Ghost Billing

    Services billed but never provided

  • Upcoding

    Billing for higher-level services than delivered

  • Unlicensed Providers

    Unqualified staff delivering services

  • Location Fraud

    Services claimed at incorrect locations
